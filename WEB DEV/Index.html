Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking, also known as penetration testing or white hat hacking, is the practice of legally and legitimately identifying vulnerabilities and weaknesses in computer systems, networks, and applications to improve their security. It involves simulating real-world cyberattacks using the same techniques and strategies employed by malicious hackers but with the explicit permission and consent of the system owners.
